Data Security

Our architectural foundation employs zero-trust principles and quantum-resistant encryption protocols. All experimental data, user telemetry, and communication logs are vaulted in decentralized, off-grid storage nodes. We maintain strict compartmentalization to ensure a localized breach cannot compromise systemic integrity.

AI Ethics

Neural network training within FAST facilities adheres to strict non-bias and transparency mandates. All generative models must maintain a documented lineage of training data. We prohibit the deployment of autonomous systems capable of executing unverified experimental procedures without secondary human oversight.

Platform Privacy Rules

User identity within the FAST network is pseudo-anonymous by default. Interaction telemetry is aggregated and stripped of identifying markers before being utilized for systemic optimization. Peer-to-peer workspace communications are end-to-end encrypted; the Institute holds no decryption keys for private lab channels.
